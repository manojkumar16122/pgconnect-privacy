1. Information We Collect

When you use PG Connect, we collect the following information:

  • Email address (used for login via OTP)
  • Phone number (optional, provided during signup)
  • Name
  • Device location (only when PG owner taps "Use My Current Location" while listing a PG)
  • Photos uploaded by PG owners for their listings
  • Payment transaction details (processed securely by Razorpay)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To display PG listings to seekers
  • To process payments for unlocking PG contact details
  • To send OTP emails for authentication
  • To allow PG owners to pin their property location on the map

3. Third-Party Services

PG Connect uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage
  • Razorpay — payment processing. We do not store card details. Razorpay handles all payment data securely.
  • Mappls Maps — for displaying PG locations on a map

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. We use Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure users can only access their own data. Passwords are never stored — we use OTP-based authentication only.

5. Contact Details Visibility

PG owner contact details (phone number and address) are hidden from seekers by default. They are only revealed after a seeker completes a ₹49 payment to unlock a specific listing. This protects owner privacy and ensures serious inquiries only.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties except as required to operate the app (Supabase, Razorpay) or as required by Indian law.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing us at manoj888310manoj@gmail.com. We will process your request within 7 business days.

8. Children's Privacy

PG Connect is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
